Commission Rules

brush

What I Will Draw

  • People (my favorite!) - Ideally 1-2 people in a given drawing. If you want a larger group photo drawn, I recommend opting for a digital drawing rather than a traditional ballpoint drawing. If you prefer traditional ballpoint for a large group portrait, you will need to opt for a larger size (like 17x22in or larger) to accommodate the detail required for multiple subjects.
  • Animals
block

What I Won't Draw

  • Explicit content, hate symbols
  • Fantasy & cartoon characters
  • Landscapes, buildings, cars, or other non-portrait subjects
timer

Turnaround Time

Turnaround time is typically 1 week for single-person 8x12 or digital portraits. Expect longer delays if requesting a drawing of more people!

check

How to Commission (Request a Drawing)

Send me a DM on Instagram or send me an email with the following information:

  • Size of drawing (8.5x12in, 11x17in, 17x22in, 22x34in, 34x44in, or digital - size is irrelevant if you want a digital drawing)
  • Reference photo(s)
  • Any specific requests or details you'd like included in the drawing

Links for Requesting a Drawing

  • DM @sketchyactivity on Instagram
  • Email austinsketches@gmail.com
attach_file

How do you get the drawing?

For traditional drawings, I will send you the drawing in the mail! I cover shipping 😊. For digital drawings, I use Procreate, and I'll send you a high-resolution image file so that you have the ability to print the drawing on a size and material of your choice. I do not pay for printing of digital drawings.

attach_money

I do not require payment until after the drawing is complete. I accept payment via Venmo or PayPal and I'll send you my info at the time of payment. I also accept cash if you prefer to meet in person. In the pricing details, "subject" refers to a person or animal in the portrait.
